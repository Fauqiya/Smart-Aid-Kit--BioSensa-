About / Problem Statement

Healthcare access in rural and semi-urban regions remains one of the most pressing challenges in India and other developing countries. Limited infrastructure, lack of diagnostic laboratories, and shortage of trained personnel often result in delayed diagnosis and inadequate treatment of common and chronic diseases. The Smart Aid Kit addresses this gap with an innovative, portable, and cost-effective solution.

Proposed Solution / System Overview

Paper-Based Diagnostic System (µPADs)

Detects multiple diseases (Glucose, Malaria, Dengue, Typhoid) with disposable paper-based tests. Sample is applied → color change → scanned via mobile app.

Health Monitoring Module

Compact module measures heart rate, oxygen saturation, and temperature, displaying results on LCD or mobile app. Easy to use for field healthcare providers.

Working Mechanism

Step 1: Start Kit

Open the Smart Aid Kit and prepare it for sample collection or monitoring.

Step 2: Collect Sample / Activate

Device collects sample or starts monitoring vital signs automatically.

Step 3: Smart Processing

Kit analyses data and generates result on the display or mobile app.

Step 4: Notification / Alert

Critical results are sent to caregivers or doctors for action.
